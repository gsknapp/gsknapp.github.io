Hi!  I'm a PIMS postdoctoral fellow in the mathematics department at the University of Calgary where I am supervised by Khoa Nguyen.  My research interests are in number theory, specifically Diophantine approximation, Thue equations, and height functions.

Prior to my employment at the University of Calgary, I earned my PhD at the University of Oregon, where I studied with Shabnam Akhtari.  I earned bachelor's degrees in math and philosophy and a master's degree in math at Case Western Reserve University (where I was advised by Colin McLarty).
